# PhantomVault Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Get Started in 5 Minutes

Welcome to PhantomVault! This quick start guide will have you protecting your folders in just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Install PhantomVault

### Linux (Ubuntu/Debian)
```bash
# Download and install
wget https://github.com/ishaq2321/phantomVault/releases/latest/phantomvault_1.0.0_amd64.deb
sudo dpkg -i phantomvault_1.0.0_amd64.deb

# Launch
phantomvault
```

### macOS
1. Download PhantomVault.dmg
2. Drag to Applications folder
3. Launch from Applications

### Windows
1. Download PhantomVault-Setup.exe
2. Run as Administrator
3. Follow installation wizard

## Step 2: Create Your First Profile

1. **Launch PhantomVault** from your applications menu
2. **Click "Create New Profile"**
3. **Enter details**:
   - Profile Name: `Personal` (or your choice)
   - Master Password: Create a strong password
   - Confirm Password: Re-enter your password
4. **Save your recovery key** (write it down safely!)
5. **Click "Create Profile"**

## Step 3: Protect Your First Folder

1. **Select your profile** and enter your master password
2. **Click "Add Folder"**
3. **Browse and select** a folder you want to protect
4. **Click "Protect Folder"**
5. **Watch the folder disappear** - it's now encrypted and hidden!

## Step 4: Access Your Protected Folders

### Method 1: Invisible Hotkey (Recommended)
1. **Press `Ctrl+Alt+V`** anywhere on your system
2. **Type your master password** within 10 seconds
3. **Your folders appear** in their original locations
4. **They auto-lock** when you lock your screen or restart

### Method 2: GUI Access
1. **Open PhantomVault**
2. **Select your profile** and authenticate
3. **Click "Unlock Temporarily"** or "Unlock Permanently"

## Step 5: Lock Folders When Done

- **Automatic**: Folders lock when you lock your screen or restart
- **Manual**: Click "Lock All Folders" in PhantomVault
- **Hotkey**: Use the invisible hotkey again to toggle lock/unlock

## 🎯 You're All Set!

Your folders are now protected with military-grade encryption and completely invisible to anyone without your password.
